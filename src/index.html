Gina O.

We couldn't be happier with our decision to use Castles in The Sky as our wedding DJ! Chris and Sarah were a pleasure to work with from the very beginning of our planning process all the way through until the conclusion of our event. Even though some changes had to be made to our original plans due to Covid, they were extremely accommodating and kept everything running smoothly. They did an amazing job reading our crowd and kept our dance floor going, despite having a smaller crowd. We loved that they not only were able to DJ our reception, but also were able to play beautiful live music for our ceremony and cocktail hour (and all at a great price)! We would definitely recommend them to anyone looking for a DJ in the area.
